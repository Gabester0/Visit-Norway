How to go to Norway

Unless you are already in Europe travelling to Norway
can be a challenge for your budget and your schedule

Fly

Pros

Flying is the fastest option available to normal people. There are lots of options to choose from. Convenience is also a big factor with flying.

Cons

Cost. Flights can be expensive and can vary unpredictably based on a variety of factors, many of which ar beyond our control

Float

Pros

Travelling across the Atlantic by boat can be a fantastic way to travel in leisurely fashion. And if you rent a cabin on a freighter it can be significantly cheaper.

Cons

Time. Travelling by boat can take an entire week. Also, if you are prone to seasickness, a week at sea might not be the best way for you to travel.
